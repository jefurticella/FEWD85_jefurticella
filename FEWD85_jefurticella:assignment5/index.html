You've made it this far. Now your reward is a bio written in third person.

Jeffrey Furticella is a Brooklyn-based sports picture editor for The New York Times. His work has been recognized by the Pictures of the Year International and National Press Photographers Association Best of Photojournalism competitions.

Previously he supervised the sports picture desk for The Associated Press in the company's New York headquarters, was a picture editor at Sporting News Magazine and initially was a photojournalist for the Times of Northwest Indiana.

He also interned at the Patuxent Publishing Company and the Muncie Star Press and attended the Eddie Adams Workshop, Barnstorm XIX.

When he is not looking for new ways to combine his love of sports and photography, he is committed to the pursuit of great pizza.
